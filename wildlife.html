🐾 Wildlife Protection

Protecting animals, forests, and biodiversity for a better future

🌿 What is Wildlife Conservation?

Wildlife conservation means protecting animals, birds, and their natural habitats. It helps maintain biodiversity, supports ecosystems, and saves endangered species like tigers, elephants, and rhinos.

🚨 Threats to Wildlife

  • Poaching – Illegal hunting for skin, horns, and bones
  • Habitat Loss – Forests cut down for farming and cities
  • Pollution – Plastic and chemicals harm animals
  • Climate Change – Rising temperatures affect migration and breeding

🌍 Why It Matters

  • Balance in Nature – Each species plays a role in the ecosystem
  • Medicinal Value – Many plants and animals help in medicine
  • Cultural Importance – Animals are part of Indian heritage
  • Tourism & Jobs – Forests and sanctuaries support livelihoods

🎓 School Activities

  • Poster Making – “Protect Tigers, Save Forests”
  • Wildlife Quiz – Learn facts about Indian animals
  • Sanctuary Visit – Explore nature and learn from experts
  • Adopt an Animal – Symbolic adoption to support rescue centers

✅ What You Can Do

  • Say No to Animal Products – Avoid leather, ivory, etc.
  • Support Sanctuaries – Donate or volunteer
  • Reduce Plastic – Keep habitats clean
  • Spread Awareness – Share facts and stories
