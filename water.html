💧 Water Conservation

Protecting every drop for people, nature, and future generations

🌊 Why Water Matters

Water is life — for humans, animals, and plants. But clean water is limited. India faces water scarcity in many regions. Saving water means saving lives.

🚨 Threats to Water

  • Wastage – Taps left open, leaking pipes
  • Pollution – Chemicals dumped in rivers
  • Overuse – Excess water in farming and homes
  • Climate Change – Less rainfall, more droughts

🌍 Impact of Water Crisis

  • Health Issues – Dirty water causes diseases
  • Crop Failure – Farmers suffer without irrigation
  • Animal Deaths – Rivers dry, habitats vanish
  • Conflicts – Water fights between states and communities

🎓 School Projects

  • Water Audit – Check how much water your school uses
  • Poster Drive – “Jal Hai Toh Kal Hai” slogans
  • Rainwater Harvesting Model – Show how to collect rain
  • Water Pledge – Class promise to save water daily

✅ What You Can Do

  • Turn off taps while brushing or washing
  • Fix leaks at home and school
  • Reuse water for plants and cleaning
  • Spread awareness with friends and family

“Every drop counts. Let’s save water today so we don’t cry tomorrow.”

Water is life — don’t waste it 💧
