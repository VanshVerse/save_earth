🔄 Renewable Energy

Clean energy for a clean future — solar, wind, and beyond

🌞 What is Renewable Energy?

Renewable energy comes from natural sources like sunlight, wind, and water. Unlike coal or petrol, it doesn’t pollute and never runs out.

🔋 Types of Clean Energy

  • Solar Power – Energy from sunlight using panels
  • Wind Energy – Turbines that spin with wind
  • Hydro Power – Dams that generate electricity
  • Biogas – Gas from waste and plants

🌍 Why It Matters

  • Less Pollution – No smoke or harmful gases
  • Saves Money – Lower bills in long term
  • Energy Security – No need to import fuel
  • Future Friendly – Safe for next generations

🎓 School Projects

  • Solar Model – Show how panels work
  • Energy Audit – Check electricity use in school
  • Poster Drive – “Switch to Solar” slogans
  • Quiz & Debate – Discuss fossil vs renewable energy

✅ What You Can Do

  • Switch off lights when not needed
  • Use LED bulbs — save energy
  • Support solar — suggest panels at school/home
  • Spread awareness — talk about clean energy
