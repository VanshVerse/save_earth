🌡️ Climate Change Awareness

Understand the crisis, its effects, and how we can protect our planet

🌍 What is Climate Change?

Climate change means long-term shifts in temperature and weather patterns. It’s caused by greenhouse gases like CO₂ from cars, factories, and deforestation. India faces heatwaves, floods, and droughts due to this crisis.

🔥 Causes of Climate Change

  • Fossil Fuels – Coal, oil, and gas release CO₂
  • Deforestation – Fewer trees to absorb carbon
  • Pollution – Smoke from vehicles and factories
  • Overconsumption – Wasteful energy and plastic use

🌪️ Effects on Earth

  • Extreme Weather – More floods, storms, and heatwaves
  • Melting Ice – Glaciers and polar ice disappearing
  • Rising Sea Levels – Coastal areas at risk
  • Health Risks – Breathing issues and heat strokes

🎓 Classroom Projects

  • Carbon Footprint Calculator – Track your daily impact
  • Climate Debate – Discuss solutions in class
  • Greenhouse Model – Show how heat gets trapped
  • Climate Art – Posters, poems, and stories

✅ What You Can Do

  • Use public transport or cycle to reduce emissions
  • Save electricity — switch off when not needed
  • Plant trees — they cool the planet
  • Speak up — awareness is the first step
